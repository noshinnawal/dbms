Pioneering Educational Tactility

EduTactile was born from a simple belief: academic management software shouldn't feel like a spreadsheet. We build neomorphic, physically responsive interfaces that bring a sense of touch and human connection back to digital administration.

flag

Our Mission

To eradicate digital fatigue in educational institutions by providing administrators, educators, and students with tools that are as intuitive and comforting to use as holding a physical notebook.

visibility

Our Vision

A future where school management systems are entirely frictionless, enabling educators to focus 100% of their energy on shaping minds rather than navigating clunky interfaces.
